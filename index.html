ABOUT ME

My art journey began in 2007, attending a small art class taught by my mom's friend. My passion for art has only grown since then, and I've worked with a multitude of mediums and teachers. Then, in high school, I began to play video games. From then on, I knew what I wanted to do for the rest of my life.

Currently, I am a junior at the Parsons School of Design in New York City. I am obtaining a BFA in Design and Technology in the Game Design pathway. My journey as a game designer has only just begun.
